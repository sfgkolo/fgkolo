ABOUT

Folklorna Grupa Kolo is a traditional Serbian dance group based in Ottawa. We are passionate about keeping the art of Serbian folklore dancing alive and thriving across borders and generations. We have performed all over Ontario at various venues and festivals. If you woud like to see beautiful traditional costumes and spirited cultural dancing, please see our next performance or join in on the fun yourself!

JOIN

If you would like a fun and cultural experience, along with some exercise and socializing, try out some folklore dancing. You don't have to be from a Serbian background to join FG Kolo, as everyone is welcome. We practice at the Serbian Center in Ottawa and would love to see you there! Please contact head choreographer Aleksandar Rajak (aco@gmail.com) for more information.
